I am very much into all genres of music. I am mostly interested in instrumental music like jam bands and jazz. I play guitar, drums, keyboards and a little bass but mostly I play guitar. I like to learn how to play classics as well as create my own songs.

I am very excited to learn and work with new technologies such as NodeJS and React. I like to learn new languages, frameworks and design patterns.

I am also a huge fan of comedy. In fact my favorite movies are comedies. Other genres include movies about real history or biographies of famous people. My favorite comediennes are Larry David, Dave Chappelle, Chris Rock, Jerry Seinfeld, Louis C.K.
